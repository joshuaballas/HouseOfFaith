WEEKEND SERVICE

The Book of Acts tells us the early believers went from house to house “breaking bread.” The words “breaking bread” was translated from a Greek phrase meaning to share a wonderful meal and a time of fellowship and relaxation with friends. With this intention, we start the evening at 6 PM sharing a pot luck meal together. This is followed by an awesome time of worship at 7 pm and a deep dose of the Word of God immediately after.

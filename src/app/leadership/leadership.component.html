LEADERSHIP

Conrad & Lisa Duncan

Senior Pastors

When I met Lisa, the Lord told me I’d marry this girl and He’d give me a ministry in a Barn. We had no idea He meant the barn in the back yard! And yet, ministry naturally took place there starting with meetings in the hay loft that lasted into the wee hours of the morning. The fog would be rolling in and dew falling, but no one wanted to leave God’s presense. Then we heard the Lord say to prepare the barn for year round ministry. The rest, as they say, is history!

Skip Smith

Associate Pastor

Pastor Skip comes from the Lebanon/Lancaster area. He came to Pittsburgh in 2006 when his grandson Raymond was born. He used his background in production management to aid in the birthing of several ministries over the years. He first attended House of Faith in 2014, where he met his future wife Dee-Dee. They were married here at The Barn in 2015 and now live in West Newton. He became Associate Pastor in 2016 and also runs a portion of the local outreach ministry.

Chris Morse

Worship Pastor

Pastor Chris, his wife Pam & son, Declan live in Forward Township PA. Chris has been our primary worship leader since 2001. After 25 years as a carpenter, God called Chris into full time ministry. He is also the Executive Director of Waypoint Youth & Community Center and oversees their outreach team Waypoint Worship. Chris and Pam work with our young adults mentoring them for service in the Kingdom.

Paul & Georgeanne Nagy

Vice President

Paul and Georgeanne are founding members of the church having ministered with the pastors since House of Faith’s inception. Pastor Isack Wanjohi

Minister of Evangelism

Pastor Isack grew up in Kenya and one day, as he tended the cows, the Lord supernaturally taught him to read English. Then He called Isack to go to America to evangelize. With little money, knowing no one, he left family and friends and crossed the ocean. He traveled several years looking for where God was calling him. When Isack arrived at the barn and saw the sign “House of Faith – Full Gospel Church” he knew he was home. Since then his radio and prison ministry has saved many.

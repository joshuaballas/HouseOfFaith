Pastor Isack Wanjohi

Minister of Evangelism

Pastor Isack Wanjohi grew up in Kenya. As a young man, he was a herdsman, keeping care of cattle. He had met a missionary who gave him a copy of an English Bible. While caring for the cattle one day, he got out the Bible and the Lord had him open it. Supernaturally, the Lord began to teach Pastor Isack to read in English. Then the Lord called him to come to the U.S. to evangelize. His anointed radio broadcast and prison ministry has seen hundreds of souls saved along with miraculous healings.
